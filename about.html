About Me

I build things people can actually use.

Durban, South Africa Advanced Diploma — In Progress 3 Projects Built Full-Stack + Mobile

I'm a software developer with a completed Diploma in Applications Development from the Durban University of Technology, currently working towards my Advanced Diploma. Whether it's a scholarship tracker helping South African students fund their studies, a mobile app connecting citizens with local authorities, or a business website with a custom admin dashboard — I like building solutions to real problems.

What drives me is the problem-solving: taking something messy and confusing and turning it into a system that just works. I'm most at home in Python and Flask on the back end, with JavaScript and Flutter rounding out the front end and mobile.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually editing video — I'm into drone footage and color grading — or being the person friends and family call when technology misbehaves. Both have taught me more about patience and debugging than any textbook.
